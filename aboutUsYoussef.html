About Us

Learn a little bit more about Youssef!

Hello! My name is Youssef, and I am one of the co-founders of Stationery Addicts. I started this online business with my partner, Anshika, to deliver the gift of artistry, organization, and all around amazing aesthetics to my local community of South Brunswick. I am an art oriented high school student, who takes multiple art classes and whose art cabinet is a different world, filled with countless art supplies. I even have an artists account on Instagram, @art_pop_non_stop. Look around our site! We hope your time here helps YOU find YOUR aesthetic.
