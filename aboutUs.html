Where it all began

Stationery Addicts started out as a local business dedicated to delivering quality stationery to our South Brunswick community. Stationery is all the rage and all about aesthetics, and we hope that your time here will immerse yourself into a world of creativity with no bounds. So what are you waiting for? Immerse yourself into a new bullet journal or a fancy ballpoint pen pack!

Our Promise

Stationery Addicts is dedicated to providing its customers with quality stationery at affordable prices with reasonable product amount. All of our products are created with care from our sources, ranging from the most skilled craftsmen in Europe to the aesthetic designers from the islands of Japan. Don't like something? Return it via mail within 30 days for a full refund or a product switch!*

* Any product switches must occur with products within the same price range and type
